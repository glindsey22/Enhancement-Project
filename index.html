CEO/Worker Pay Ratio, 2014

The purpose of this visualization is to shed light on the disproportionate amount of money most CEOs in American companies make when compared to the average salary of their respective employees. The median ratio between CEO and average employee pay is 197:1, it can be observed that after this mark the average CEO pay increases significantly, as can be seen in the increased radii of the circles. Note that the x-axis is approximate, positions may vary due to the force simulation.

Companies based on the S&P 500
